Our Story

Charity Water is a nonprofit organization dedicated to bringing clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries. Founded in 2006, we believe that access to clean water is a basic human right. Our mission is to end the global water crisis by funding sustainable water projects around the world.

703 million people lack basic access to clean and safe drinking water. Worldwide, women and girls are responsible for collecting water in 80% of households. When a community receives clean water women get hours back in their day and take charge of their own futures. Less time collecting water means more time in class. With access to clean water, kids around the world could get 443 million school days back every year. Every $1 invested in clean water can yield $4.30 in economic returns. Access to clean water is perhaps the single most powerful investment for sparking economic growth humanity has ever known. Access to clean water and basic sanitation can save around 16,000 lives every week.
